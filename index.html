FourGate

Most farm decisions are made on habit, neighbour advice, and last season's price — even when the capital, land, and water committed are irreversible.

FourGate is a four-gate planning system that takes a farm from raw conditions to a clear grow / don't grow decision — with a kill / proceed check at every gate.

Works for any crop, any country, any scale. Complete all sections honestly. The verdict is only as good as your inputs.

Every agricultural season has three stages — planning, growing, and harvest.

Find a problem at planning: fix it by switching crops, adjusting inputs, or securing a better buyer.
Find it during the growing season: it costs money and reduces yield but can still be managed.
Find it at harvest: the loss is already done. Nothing is recoverable.

This framework exists for the planning stage.
Use it before you buy seed, hire labour, or commit water.
Planning is the only stage where failure is still cheap to correct.
